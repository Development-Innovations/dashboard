Digital M&E Support

Implementing Partner: Cooperation Committee for Cambodia (CCC)

Design

Research and learning to develop a replicable and sustainable Digital M&E Support Package that equips Phnom Penh and provincial CSOs with the ability to efficiently collect, manage and conduct basic visualization of M&E data with phone/mobile/cloud-based data management systems.

  • Partners: USAID, YouthStar Cambodia
  • Technology Solution: Digital M&E Tool
  • Direct Beneficiaries: 2,000 Users
