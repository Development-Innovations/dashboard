Technology for Education Systems Transformation (TEST)

Under TRAC, World Education has already created an mLearning app that corresponds with the national reading textbooks. Through this experience, World Education began to think of other ways that technology could be used to improve the teaching and learning process. Given the challenges TRAC faced with teacher administration of the CAS, as well as the fact that World Education has already provided 20 tablets to each TRAC school, improving the CAS through technology seemed a fitting extension of the TRAC project. Thus, TEST proposes to digitize and automate the TRAC CAS in order to promote teacher understanding and use of the CAS as a diagnostic tool for student learning.

Simultaneously, the CAS App will facilitate teacher and student use and understanding of technology in the classroom.

Deploy
  • Grantee: World Education
  • Partners: USAID Cambodia
  • Technical Providers: CamMob
  • Technology Solution: Android Based Learning Tools
  • Direct Beneficiaries: 2,700 Users
